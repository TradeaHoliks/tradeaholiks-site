Licensing, in plain English

How does the licence work?

Each licence is tied to your computer. You enter your key once, the Journal activates it, and it quietly checks it's still valid each time you open the app. If your internet drops, it keeps working for a grace period so you're never locked out by a blip.

Can I move it to a new PC?

Yes. Licences can be released from an old machine and activated on a new one — you're not stuck if you upgrade your hardware.

What's the difference between the plans?

They're the same app. Monthly and annual are subscriptions; lifetime is a single payment you keep forever. Annual works out cheaper than monthly, and lifetime is the best deal if you plan to stick around.
