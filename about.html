// ABOUT

Built at the desk, not in a boardroom.

Tradeaholiks makes the tools we wished existed while we were trading — practical, accurate, and quietly powerful.

// The idea

Trading is hard enough

The admin around it shouldn't be. Most journals are either a blank spreadsheet you have to feed by hand, or bloated software that buries the numbers you actually care about. We wanted something in between: a tool that captures the data on its own and shows the truth without fuss.

So we built it for our own desk first — then polished it into something we'd be happy to put in front of other traders.
